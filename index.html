🚃 Train Line Cheat Sheet

JR Yamanote
Green loop — Shinjuku↔Shibuya↔Harajuku↔Akihabara↔Ueno↔Ikebukuro
JR Chuo (Rapid)
Orange — Tokyo↔Shinjuku↔Koenji↔Kichijoji. Fast cross-city.
JR Chuo-Sobu (Local)
Yellow — Shinjuku↔Akihabara↔Ryogoku. All stops version.
Marunouchi Line
Red — Shinjuku↔Ginza↔Tokyo Station↔Ikebukuro.
Tozai Line
Light blue — Nakano↔Kudanshita↔Nihombashi.
Chiyoda Line
Green — Omotesando↔Nezu↔Sendagi. Connects to Harajuku area.
Oedo Line
Magenta — Shinjuku↔Roppongi↔Tsukiji↔Ryogoku. Deep underground.
Keikyu Line
Blue — Haneda Airport↔Shinagawa. Your airport transfer line.
Ginza Line
Orange — Shibuya↔Ginza↔Asakusa. Oldest line, very useful.
Yurikamome
Automated — Shimbashi↔Odaiba↔Toyosu. For Ultra Music Festival!
💡 Look for these colors on station signs and maps. IC card works on ALL of them.

📱 Connectivity

eSIM (recommended): Ubigi, Airalo, or Mobal. Activate before boarding. 10GB plan ~RM90.

Free WiFi: 7-Eleven, Starbucks, train stations. Download "Japan Wi-Fi auto-connect" app.

Pocket WiFi: Rent at Haneda if group travel (¥500-900/day). Unlimited data, share with 5 devices.
